# Pitfall: Sakai Chemical (4078.T) Liquidity Trap

**Material leaders are useless if they aren't tradable on retail brokers (TR/DKB).**

## Symptom
Agents identify Sakai Chemical as the #1 bottleneck play for MLCC Barium Titanate powder but fail to verify its tradability, leading to non-actionable investment recommendations.

## Why
Sakai Chemical is a small-to-mid-cap Japanese material leader. It lacks the secondary listings on the Lang & Schwarz (LS) or Tradegate exchanges that German neobrokers (Trade Republic, DKB) require for execution. Even if a ticker appears, "Buy" orders may be permanently disabled.

## How to avoid
1.  **Verify First:** Always check the ISIN `JP3312800000` against the specific broker's available instrument list before performing deep-dive analysis.
2.  **Use Vertical Proxies:** Instead of the material specialist, recommend vertically integrated leaders or those with secured supply JVs:
    *   **Murata (JP3914400001):** Produces own ceramic powder via JVs.
    *   **TDK (JP3538800008):** High-reliability material integration.
3.  **Health Basket Placement:** If the material leader is the "bottleneck," place it in the **Health Basket** of the thesis to track the signal, but never in the **Tradable Proxies**.

## Counter-example
Allocating 40% of a portfolio to Sakai Chemical in a THESIS.md intended for a Trade Republic user. The user will be unable to open the position, rendering the thesis "cold" and ineffective.
